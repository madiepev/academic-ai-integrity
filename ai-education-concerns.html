Everyone Is Worried About the Risks

Student and teacher concerns across diverse themes

As artificial intelligence integrates into academic life, different groups perceive different risks. Students and teachers show different patterns of concern and confidence. This visualization maps those patterns across key themes, showing how proximity to AI reflects the depth of concern. The closer to the rocket, the less concerned — and vice versa.
