Academic AI Integrity

A shared starting point for students and teachers

Generative AI has become part of academic life — over 90% of students now use AI tools, yet consistent norms and a shared language remain rare across programmes. This course builds that foundation: connecting what AI is, how to use it thoughtfully, how to evaluate its outputs, and how to act with integrity. Designed for both students and teachers, it culminates in two authentic artefacts — a course-ready AI rationale for teachers and an assignment-ready disclosure for students.

Course design

These principles shape every decision in the course — from module sequence to artefact design. Hover each numbered node to see the principle and its rationale.

Mapping

Design principles → course elements

Each design principle underpins a specific element of the course. Principles 1–3 form the Skills & Literacy zone and ground the three active learning modules. Principles 4–6 form the Integrity & Responsibility zone and anchor the policy and reflective elements of the course.
